About Health Hub

Inspiration:
Health Hub is the culmination of my personal journey towards holistic wellness. Faced with the challenges of finding reliable health information and resources, I set out to create a solution that would empower others on their own wellness journeys.

Timeline:
This project is a testament to my dedication and passion for both web development and wellness. Developed as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, it represents countless hours of learning, experimentation, and refinement.
